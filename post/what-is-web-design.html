what is web design?

Web design is the art and the science of making your website look great, and be easy to use.

The main purpose of web design is to make your site function, as well as possible.

Many people consider web design an aesthetic pursuit of making the site look as nice and appealing as possible.

However, if you want your website to attract customers and generate revenue, there must be a solid emphasis on user experience.

The goal of web design is not just to create a web presence for you, but to give Internet users an easy way to connect with your business.

what is web designer?

A professional of the creative industry who mainly focuses on the art of creating and designing websites, which are mechanisms that allow people to find information and products online.

The web designer develops the plan for a website using his skills, talent, imagination and creativity.

The Web designer refers to the person who makes the design for a website.
